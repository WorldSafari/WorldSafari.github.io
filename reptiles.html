Take the Reptiles Tour!

Reptiles are cold-blooded animals and are covered in scales. The scales on their body are used as protection. These scales come in all shapes and sizes. protects their bodies and can be big or small, hard or soft. These animals are greatly affected by temperature and the environment around them. If they become too hot, they go find a cooler place to rest. If they are too cold, they try to find a warmer area. Go on the adventure to learn about specific reptiles.
