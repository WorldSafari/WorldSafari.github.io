Welcome to World Safari! Our world is made up of different types of animals that help make the world as exciting as it is. Animals are grouped into different categories and this makes up the animal kingdom.

The animal kingdom is broken up into phyla. Each phylum is broken up into different classes. Each class is broken up into different orders. Each order is broken up into different families. Each family is broken up into different genera. Each genus is broken up into different species. A species is the smallest classification of a type of animal. Throughout the site, we will be taking you on a journey and learning about different species of animals that live in our world.
